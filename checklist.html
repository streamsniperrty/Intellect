CHECKLIST!

Applying to college is like a game! To maximize your best efforts, we have
provided a checklist you can use to make the most out of each grade
since your freshman year of high school.

Freshman:

  • Familiarize yourself with your teachers.
  • Explore clubs and pick out ones to stick to for all four years.
  • Take up AP classes and the AP tests.
  • Take up summer extracurriculars and/or college courses. (Best if related to intended major)
  • Freshman year counts! Do not slack.

Sophomore:

  • Take the PSAT.
  • Take up AP classes and the AP tests.
  • Aim for leadership positions in clubs.
  • Take up summer extracurriculars and/or college courses. (Best if related to intended major)

Junior:

  • Take the SAT and/or the ACT.
  • Take up AP classes and the AP tests.
  • Forge great bonds with your teachers and ask for recommendation letters near the end of spring semester.
  • Dedicate time to last set of summer extracurriculars.
  • Utilize summer to completing your college personal statement/essay.
  • Start forming your college list.

Senior:

  • Finalize college essay if applying Early Decision and/or Early Action. (Deadline by November)
  • Finalize your college list and start applying for Regular Decision (Deadline by January)
  • Start applying to scholarships.
  • First semester counts! Don't slack.
