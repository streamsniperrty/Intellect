MISSION STATEMENT

With Intellect, we aim to simplify the college application process for high school students. As fellow applicants, we understand the challenges of managing multiple applications and deadlines. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that consolidates all necessary information in one place, from by grade checklists, direction to resources, and more. We empower students with the tools and knowledge to approach the college application process confidently, making higher education more accessible for all.
